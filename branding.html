Cumberland Veterinary Hospital Branding

Branding, February 2018

I had the opportunity to create a new branding identity for Cumberland Veterinary Hospital. They are a very small, family-owned company in Orleans. It was important that this be reflected in their new logo. The new design represents the friendly and welcoming atmosphere of the office. The two animals leaning into each other represents the perfect image of warmth the customer needs to feel comfortable. Also, maintaining the use of the burgundy stays true to the brand the clients have come to know and love. Overall, this refreshing look better represents this small company's core values.
