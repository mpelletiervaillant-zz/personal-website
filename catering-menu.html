CATERING MENU

Print Design & Typography, 2017

This past summer, I was asked to design a simple catering menu for a family-owned restaurant called OCCO Kitchen. The client wanted something simple and easy to print. I decided to match the restaurant's already established esthetics and used a gothic, bold all caps font. The body copy was set in Lato to contrast the titles.

This design is super simple to assemble. Print then fold, and you have a booklet ready to go!

The client also wanted photographs of the actual product, so these were incorporated for a more genuine design.
