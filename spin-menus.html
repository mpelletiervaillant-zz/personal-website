"SPIN"
Kitchen & Bar

Layout, May 2017

I had the pleasure of creating "SPIN" Kitchen & Bar's variety of menus. They searched for a modern and clean look, yet wanted something versatile that could be set for different types of menus. The simple black text on white makes reading easy. The narrow font choice gives an edge but remains formal with the use of a serif font for the body copy. This style has been applied for all of their menus.
